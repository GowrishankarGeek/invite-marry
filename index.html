Day One

Date: 2nd year first day
I Was Scared
God knows how it all happened ,me returning from home. I am still thankful to him for the perfect timing and that beautiful day when I first saw her.I literally stood still looking at her ,posing with my shades hoping to catch her attention but she passed by..not even slightest of bhao....I was left with just a hope to keep myself happy.." dekhi to thi ek baar "..I hadn't slept that night. I did feel that aaj main upar aasman neeche wali feeling..eager and restless for the next morning when the bus would come and I would forget everything and watch her, trying to get myself noticed ....Yes I didn't have the courage to approach her...but was always determined to make her mine since that very moment. I still feel the same when I see her ,still become as excited n go crazy when she tells me now ,that I had seen her that day in there. I will always feel the same. she is the only thing that I have earned....These long years have finally turned into hours....
Eswaran
